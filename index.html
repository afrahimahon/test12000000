অমর একুশে
Language, Identity & Pride

21st February 1952 is an unforgettable and glorious day for the Bengali nation and all Bangla-speaking people. On this day, the brave students and general people of then East Bengal took to the streets to protect their mother tongue, Bangla, standing firmly against injustice and conspiracy.

Their sacrifice not only safeguards the Bangla language but also teaches the world that language is the foundation of a nation's identity, culture, and existence. Today, 21st February is not only significant for Bengalis, but also for people of all languages around the world.

We have created this video as a humble tribute to the Language Martyrs, expressing our deep respect and gratitude. Their sacrifice is our inspiration and our pride.

Our Story

About Us

01 Prepway Bangladesh Est. Aug 9, 2025

Born from one urgent question — why are millions of Bangladeshi students navigating SSC, HSC, and university admissions completely alone?

The answer came after the first episode of the Prepway Podcast, where Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon hosted a discussion on studying abroad without IELTS or SAT. The response was overwhelming — students flooded in with local struggles too: admission prep, subject guidance, life after HSC.

Ahon raised the idea with the other directors. Together, they built Prepway Bangladesh — a platform dedicated to every stage of a student's academic journey within the country. It began with podcasts, because the most powerful guidance comes from students who have already been through it. Show Your Voice became that series — unscripted, real, and built entirely for Bangladesh.

02 Prepway Abroad Est. Jun 26, 2025

Before Prepway Bangladesh, before Prepway Group — four people started something independently to help Bangladeshi students reach the world.

Prepway Abroad was built as a podcast and content platform covering real student stories from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and beyond — scholarships, visas, university applications, IELTS, and life abroad. The founding team:

K. M. Rubaid Ferdous Swapnil
Zinat Hossain Any
Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon
Md. Emran Hasan Emon

At founding, Prepway Abroad stood alone — no group, no umbrella. Just four people with a mission. That mission resonated, grew, and ultimately sparked the idea for Prepway Bangladesh.

03 Prepway Group Est. Aug 2025

Two growing initiatives. One shared vision. Prepway Group was the natural result of growth — not the starting point.

Once Prepway Bangladesh launched in August 2025, it became clear both initiatives needed a shared identity and direction. Prepway Group was formed as the parent organisation — bringing Prepway Bangladesh and Prepway Abroad under one roof.

Today the group is committed to one purpose: empowering every Bangladeshi student, whether preparing for their HSC at home or applying to universities on the other side of the world.
